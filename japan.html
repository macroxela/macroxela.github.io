    • The land of the rising sun, Japan has fascinated me since I was a toddler. Something about it simply allured me. My original plans at the time were to go to Ukraine and Russia but since those plans fell through I decided to go to Japan. I spent about a month and a half, from mid July to early August. I started off in Yakushima then explored Kagoshima, Nagasaki, Fukuoka, Hiroshima, Osaka, Kyoto, Himeji, Tokushima, Kamojima, Nagoro, and Tokyo.
