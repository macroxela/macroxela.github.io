    • My second road trip in the U.S. During Spring Break 2018 a friend and I decided to drive to Washington DC. It was his first time travelling for fun and my first trip to the U.S. capital. I've been at least 6 other capitals yet not to my own country's. We spent most of the week exploring the various Smithsonian museums and monuments. On our way back we stopped by the Steven F. Udvar-Hazy Center to check out the huge collection of aircraft. It was also my first experience using an Airbnb which turned out to be much better than I expected.
