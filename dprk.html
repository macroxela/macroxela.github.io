    • This was the place I selected as my first location to travel abroad. I wasn't even aware one could travel to the DPRK until a few months before my trip. Why the DPRK as my first location abroad? One, curiosity. It simply appealed to me how different the DPRK would be from any other country. We hear so much about it yet know so little. Two, it would push me past my comfort zone in a way few other countries can. This was going to be my 1st trip abroad and I wanted to visit someplace that would push my boundaries. The enigma of North Korea offerred just that; a mysterious country which many people fear and judge due to the political climate. I booked a weeklong trip through Koryo Tours since it is one of the first and more reputable tour companies to North Korea. The trip took place from 2 July to 9 July 2016. We explored Pyongyang and the southern parts of North Korea along Kaesong & Kumgang in addition to the east coast along Hamhung.
