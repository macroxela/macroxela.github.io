    • Although most people go to Machu Picchu by train or bus, what attracted me more was the possibility of hiking through the Andes to Machu Picchu. Instead of trekking the Inca trail I decided to hike the Salkantay trek. The Salkantay trek is a less trekked (although recently increasing in popularity) trail taking one through Salkantay pass at 4600 m altitude.
